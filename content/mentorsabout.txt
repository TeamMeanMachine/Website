
About our Mentors
<br><br>


FIRST Robotics Team 2471 students work with engineering and business mentors to guide them as they design and build their robots and market the team. Our mentors received Community Leadership Awards in 2013 from the Washington Association of School Administrators for the volunteer work with our team and in the community.
